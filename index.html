    • Full Name: [Your Full Name]

    Account Deletion Process:

    Once we receive your account deletion request, our support team will review it. If there are no pending issues or unresolved matters associated with your account, we will initiate the account deletion process. Please be aware of the following:

    1. Account Deletion Timeframe: The account deletion process may take up to 30 days to complete.
    2. Inaccessibility During Deletion: During the deletion process, you may not be able to access your account or use the app's services.
    3. Confirmation Email: Once your account has been successfully deleted, you will receive a confirmation email to notify you of the completion of the process.
    4. Pending Issues: If there are any pending issues, such as unresolved appointments or disputes, your account deletion may be delayed until these issues are resolved.

    Retention of Email Address for Payment-related Reasons:

    Please note that, for legitimate payment-related reasons, we may retain your email address in our archives even after your account has been deleted. This retention is solely for the purpose of ensuring accurate payment records and may be used for future reference in case of payment-related inquiries or issues.
